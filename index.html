____________________HAIR CATEGORIES____________________

Wavy Hair

Give your look a fresh update with our beautiful wavy hairstyle that adds lovely volume.These soft waves frame your face perfectly, giving you a relaxed and stylish feel. Whether you’re out with friends or dressing up for a special event, this hairstyle will highlight your natural beauty and make you shine.

Ponytail

Elevate your look instantly with our specialized Ponytail Hair Sets! At Be A.Geelized, we recognize that the ponytail is the ultimate versatile hairstyle—chic, sophisticated, and perfect for any occasion, from the office to the runway.

Half-up

The Half-Up Hair Set is the mixture of balance and effortless chic—it keeps your hair beautifully styled and secured while allowing your length and texture to flow freely. At Be A.Geelized, we perfect this timeless look, transforming it from a casual style into a sophisticated statement suitable for any special occasion.

Up-do

For moments that demand formal perfection and lasting glamour, the Updo Hair Set offered at Be A.Geelized is the ultimate choice. Our updos are not just about pinning hair up; they are carefully sculpted works of art designed to highlight your facial features, accentuate your gown, and ensure you maintain a polished, impeccable look from the first moment to the last.

____________________MAKE-UP CATEGORIES____________________

Soft-Glam

Our Soft Glam Makeup Look is the perfect synthesis of a sophisticated, camera-ready finish and a subtle, natural glow. It's the ideal choice when you want to feel polished and glamorous without the heavy, dramatic feel of full coverage or harsh lines. Full-Glam

Our Full Glam Makeup Look is designed to deliver a striking, high-impact appearance that is absolutely flawless under all lighting conditions, especially professional photography and evening lights. This is our boldest, most defined, and most glamorous service—perfect for the client who wants to turn heads and feel like they just stepped off a red carpet.
